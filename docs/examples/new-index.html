Pupp- What?

Puppertino is a CSS/JS framework based on the Human guidelines and UIs of iOS and macOS.

Puppertino is made for people who want to develop websites, apps, and everything with the iconic Apple style without wanting to spend more time coding the UI.

This doesn't mean that Puppertino is a direct rip-off of all the components of Apple. Instead, we've focused on creating what we think are the most used components.

Customize Puppertino

Based on powerful CSS variables Puppertino offers you a wide range of customization. Import just what you need, and customiza colors and values with the changes of simple CSS variables.

With a wide range of colors, classes, and options to use, customizing Puppertino is done in a breeze, so you can adapt it to the look of your brand and needs.
